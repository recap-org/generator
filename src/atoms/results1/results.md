We estimate the following linear model:

$$
\text{exam\_score}_i = \beta_0 + \beta_1 \text{hours\_studied}_i + \beta_2 \text{sleep\_hours}_i
+ \beta_3 \text{attendance\_percent}_i + \beta_4 \text{previous\_scores}_i + \varepsilon_i,
$$

where $\varepsilon_i$ denotes an idiosyncratic error term. @lst-table reports the estimated coefficients, and @lst-figure visualizes them with 95% confidence intervals.
